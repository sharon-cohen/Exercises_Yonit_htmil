Pauda

Pauda ("Chaos" in Arabic) tells the story of an undercover team led by Doron Kabilio (Lior Raz).

The main task of the undercover forces is to damage the Hamas infrastructure in the Gaza-operated West Bank and to reach the top of the organization's military arm in the Gaza Strip.
